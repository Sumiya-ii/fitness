# Phase 1: Product Definition

## 1) App single-sentence promise

Coach helps Mongolian users lose fat by making food logging fast and delivering clear daily calorie and macro guidance they can actually follow.

## 2) Exact target user for v1

Mongolian-speaking adults (roughly 20-40) who want fat loss, use Telegram daily, and have previously failed to stay consistent with manual calorie tracking.

## 3) Primary transformation the app delivers

Users move from inconsistent, guess-based eating to consistent, measured eating that reliably hits calorie and protein targets most days of the week.

## 4) Main daily habit to create

Log every calorie-containing meal/snack within 10 minutes of eating, then check remaining calories/macros before the day ends.

## 5) One weekly outcome users should feel

"I was in control this week": at least 5 logged days, clear protein adherence, and a visible weight trend moving toward goal.

## 6) Top 3 reasons users would churn

1. Logging feels slow or high-friction compared with their daily routine.
2. Local food search/barcode results are missing or inaccurate.
3. Progress feedback is unclear, so effort does not feel connected to outcomes.

## 7) v1 scope and explicit out-of-scope

In scope (v1):
- Auth + onboarding + target setup
- Food logging (text, quick add, barcode, voice draft, photo draft)
- Daily dashboard + weight trend + weekly summary
- Telegram linking, reminders, and basic coaching prompts
- Free/pro entitlement gating

Out of scope (v1):
- Medical diagnosis or treatment advice
- Fully automatic AI logging without confirmation
- Adaptive metabolism engine and advanced personalized planning
- Full wearable ecosystem integrations

## 8) Business model choice

Hybrid: free core tracking + paid subscription for AI-heavy and advanced coaching features.

## 9) Product posture

Both: tracker-like at the core (reliable logging + trends), coach-like in delivery (daily guidance and accountability).

## 10) Core v1 success metric

Primary metric: Weekly Active Loggers-5 (WAL-5) = percentage of active users who log at least 2 meals on at least 5 days in a week.
