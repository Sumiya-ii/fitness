## Design Guidelines for All Posters

### Brand Colors
- Primary: [Use Coach app primary color]
- Accent: Warm orange/amber (food-friendly)
- Background: Dark mode preferred (more premium, matches app)
- Text: White on dark, dark on light

### Typography
- Mongolian text: Clear, readable sans-serif font
- Numbers/stats: Bold, large -- calorie numbers should be the biggest text
- CTA: Contrasting color button/banner

### Photos
- Food photos should be appetizing and well-lit
- Use real Mongolian food (not stock photos of Western food)
- Phone mockups should show actual app screenshots
- People in photos should look Mongolian

### Sizing
- Instagram feed: 1080x1080 or 1080x1350
- Instagram story: 1080x1920
- Facebook: 1200x630
- TikTok thumbnail: 1080x1920
- Print (gym): A3 or A4

### Tools
- Canva (primary -- see canva_prompts.md for templates)
- Figma (if more control needed)
- Phone mockup generators: mockuphone.com, Canva frames
