## Platform-Specific Notes

### TikTok
- Hook must hit in first 0.5 seconds
- Text overlay essential (most watch without sound)
- End CTA: "Link in bio" or "Search Coach on App Store"
- No clickable links in description (only bio link)

### Instagram Reels
- Hook in first 1 second
- CTA in caption + end card
- Use "link in bio" format
- Save-worthy content gets algorithm boost

### Facebook
- Longer hooks OK (first line of caption)
- Direct links in posts work
- Comments and shares matter most
- Facebook Groups for community CTAs

### Instagram Stories
- Swipe-up (if available) or "link in bio"
- Polls, quizzes, and sliders for engagement
- "DM me" CTAs for personal touch
- "Share to your story" for reach
