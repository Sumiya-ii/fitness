# Coach -- 5 Strategic Content Pillars

> Each pillar includes: description, audience segment, content formats, example posts, posting frequency, and platform priority.

---

## Pillar 1: "Зураг авч калори тоол" (Snap & Count -- AI Magic)

### Description
Showcase the hero feature: snap a photo of any food and instantly get calorie + macro breakdown. This is the "wow factor" content that drives awareness and downloads. Every video should make the viewer think: "I need to try this."

### Target Audience
All segments -- this is the universal hook

### Content Formats
- **Reel/TikTok (primary)**: Point camera at food -> app scans -> numbers reveal
- **Carousel**: Before (food photo) / After (calorie breakdown) side-by-side
- **Story**: Quick daily "scan of the day"
- **Live**: "Та ямар хоолны калори мэдмээр байна? Коммент бич!" (What food's calories do you want to know? Comment!)

### Example Posts

1. **Reel**: Camera zooms into a plate of buuz. Text: "6 бууз = ??? ккал". App scans. Reveal: "456 ккал, 28г уураг". Reaction face.
2. **Reel**: Walking into a restaurant, ordering tsuivan, scanning it before eating. "Цуйвангийн калори шалгая!"
3. **Carousel**: 5 slides, each a different Mongolian food with calorie count revealed
4. **Story**: "Өглөөний цай скан" (Morning tea scan) -- daily ritual content
5. **TikTok**: Speed-run: scan 5 different foods in 30 seconds, rapid-fire calorie reveals

### Posting Frequency
3-4x per week across platforms

### Platform Priority
TikTok > Instagram Reels > Facebook Video

### Key Metrics
Views, shares, saves, "How do I download?" comments

## Pillar 2: "Монгол хоолны шим тэжээл" (Mongolian Food Nutrition Facts)

### Description
Educational content about the nutrition content of Mongolian foods. This fills a genuine knowledge gap -- there's almost no accessible data about calories in buuz, khuushuur, tsuivan, aaruul, etc. Position Coach as the authority on Mongolian food nutrition.

### Target Audience
Segment 1 (Beginners), Segment 3 (Weight Loss), Segment 4 (Health-Conscious)

### Content Formats
- **Carousel (primary)**: "Х хоолонд хэдэн калори байдаг?" fact cards
- **Reel/TikTok**: Side-by-side calorie comparisons, "which has more?" quiz format
- **Infographic**: Mongolian food calorie chart (shareable, saveable)
- **Facebook post**: Long-form nutrition fact with engagement question

### Example Posts

1. **Carousel**: "Монгол хоолны калори гайд"
   - Slide 1: Бууз (6 ширхэг) = 456 ккал
   - Slide 2: Хуушуур (3 ширхэг) = 510 ккал
   - Slide 3: Цуйван (1 таваг) = 480 ккал
   - Slide 4: Банштай шөл (1 аяга) = 320 ккал
   - Slide 5: "Coach апп-аар бүгдийг нь тоол"

2. **Reel**: "Бууз vs Хуушуур -- аль нь илүү калоритай?" Quiz format with dramatic reveal.

3. **Infographic**: "Цагаан сарын ширээний калори" (Tsagaan Sar table calories) -- buuz, aaruul, boortsog, suutei tsai, airag side by side

4. **Facebook post**: "Та нэг хоолоор хэдэн калори авдгаа мэдэх үү? Олон монгол хоол таны бодсоноос олон калоритай байдаг. Жишээ нь..." (Do you know how many calories you get from one meal? Many Mongolian foods have more calories than you think. For example...)

5. **Carousel**: "Оффис хоол vs Гэрийн хоол -- калорийн ялгаа" (Office lunch vs home food -- calorie difference)

### Posting Frequency
2-3x per week

### Platform Priority
Instagram Carousels > Facebook Posts > TikTok

### Key Metrics
Saves (indicator of value), shares, comments ("Үнэн үү??")

## Pillar 3: "Fitness Journey" (Transformation & Motivation)

### Description
Real user stories, progress tracking, and motivation content. This pillar builds social proof and emotional connection. Even small wins matter: "I logged food for 7 days straight" is a transformation story.

### Target Audience
Segment 2 (Active Gym-Goers), Segment 3 (Weight Loss)

### Content Formats
- **Reel/TikTok**: Before/after with Coach dashboard screenshots
- **Carousel**: "My first 30 days with Coach" journey posts
- **Story**: Daily check-in screenshots, streak celebrations
- **Facebook post**: Long-form user testimonials
- **UGC**: Reshare user-posted content

### Example Posts

1. **Reel**: Split screen -- left: user's Day 1 dashboard (empty). Right: Day 30 dashboard (full rings, 30-day streak). Audio: motivational Mongolian song.

2. **Carousel**: User spotlight
   - Slide 1: User photo + quote: "Би 30 хоног хоолоо бүртгэсэн"
   - Slide 2: Their dashboard screenshot
   - Slide 3: What they learned
   - Slide 4: Their tip for beginners
   - Slide 5: Download CTA

3. **TikTok**: "1 сар Coach хэрэглээд юу өөрчлөгдсөн" (What changed after 1 month using Coach) -- POV style

4. **Facebook**: Long testimonial post from a real user (with permission). "Би өмнө нь калори тоолж байгаагүй. Coach апп хэрэглэж эхлээд..."

5. **Story series**: "7 хоногийн challenge" (7-day challenge) -- daily updates from a participant

### Posting Frequency
1-2x per week

### Platform Priority
Facebook Posts > Instagram Reels > TikTok

### Key Metrics
Engagement rate, DMs asking "how do I start?", app downloads attributed to testimonial content

## Pillar 4: "Монгол хоолоор Fitness" (Fitness with Mongolian Food)

### Description
Practical content showing that you DON'T need to eat chicken breast and broccoli to be fit. Mongolian food can absolutely be part of a fitness diet -- you just need to know the portions and macros. This pillar breaks the myth that fitness requires "foreign" food.

### Target Audience
Segment 2 (Active Gym-Goers), Segment 4 (Health-Conscious)

### Content Formats
- **Reel/TikTok**: "Монгол хоолоор bulk/cut хийх" (Bulk/cut with Mongolian food)
- **Carousel**: Meal plans using Mongolian food with calorie counts
- **Video**: "What I eat in a day -- Mongolian edition" with Coach tracking
- **Facebook post**: Tips on making traditional food healthier

### Example Posts

1. **Reel**: "Bulk хийх монгол хоол" (Mongolian food for bulking)
   - Show: Big plate of tsuivan scanned -> high protein, high cal
   - Show: Бууз + тараг combo -> protein-rich
   - Text: "Чикен брист хэрэггүй шүү"

2. **Carousel**: "Монгол хоолны өдрийн хоолны төлөвлөгөө -- 2000 ккал"
   - Breakfast: Сүүтэй цай + талх + өндөг (350 ккал)
   - Lunch: Бууз 4ш + зеленый шөл (400 ккал)
   - Dinner: Цуйван хагас порц (300 ккал)
   - Snacks: Тараг + жимс (250 ккал)
   - Each scanned with Coach

3. **TikTok**: "3 монгол хоол -- уургаар баялаг" (3 Mongolian foods rich in protein)
   - Each food scanned with Coach, protein highlighted

4. **Facebook**: "Fitness хийхийн тулд гадаадын хоол идэх шаардлагагүй. Монгол хоолоороо калори тоолоод, зорилгодоо хүрч болно." (You don't need foreign food for fitness. Count calories with Mongolian food and reach your goals.)

5. **Carousel**: "Кафений хоол vs Гэрийн хоол -- хоёуланг нь Coach-оор тоол" (Cafe food vs home food -- track both with Coach)

### Posting Frequency
2x per week

### Platform Priority
Instagram Reels > TikTok > Facebook

### Key Metrics
Saves, shares to fitness/food groups, recipe-request comments

## Pillar 5: "Дасгал & Урам зориг" (Workout & Motivation)

### Description
Workout content, fitness tips, and daily motivation. Connects Coach's workout tracking feature with nutrition. Also serves as general engagement content that keeps the audience warm between product-focused posts.

### Target Audience
Segment 2 (Active Gym-Goers), Segment 1 (Beginners)

### Content Formats
- **Reel/TikTok**: Quick workout demos, gym clips
- **Story**: Daily motivation quotes, workout + food logging combos
- **Carousel**: Workout routine + recommended daily calories
- **Facebook post**: Motivational content, "Today's workout" prompts

### Example Posts

1. **Reel**: Film a workout, then immediately log post-workout meal with Coach. "Дасгалын дараа хоолоо бүртгэ" (Log your food after workout)

2. **TikTok**: "Монгол gym-чин ийм хоол идвэл" (If a Mongolian gym-goer eats this) -- scan a big post-workout meal

3. **Carousel**: "Дасгал + Хоолны хослол"
   - Slide 1: Chest day workout
   - Slide 2: Post-chest day meal scanned in Coach (high protein)
   - Slide 3: Leg day workout
   - Slide 4: Post-leg day meal scanned
   - Slide 5: "Coach-оор дасгал, хоол хоёуланг нь track хий"

4. **Story**: Morning motivation + ring progress screenshot: "Өглөө 7:00 -- зорилго тодорхой, эхэлцгээе!" (7 AM -- goals clear, let's start!)

5. **Reel**: "30 хоногийн бие бялдарын challenge -- Coach-тойгоо хамт" (30-day fitness challenge -- together with Coach)

### Posting Frequency
1-2x per week

### Platform Priority
TikTok > Instagram Reels > Facebook

### Key Metrics
Views, profile visits, challenge participation

## Pillar Integration Rules

1. **Every post should have a CTA** -- either download, try the feature, or engage (comment/save/share)
2. **Cross-pollinate pillars** -- a "Mongolian food" post can feature the AI scan; a "workout" post can show meal logging
3. **The AI photo scan should appear in 60%+ of all video content** -- it is the hook that drives downloads
4. **User-generated content can fit any pillar** -- always reshare with credit
5. **Seasonal content gets priority** -- Tsagaan Sar, Naadam, New Year override regular pillar schedule
