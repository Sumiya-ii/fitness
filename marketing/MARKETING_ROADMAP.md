# Coach Marketing Roadmap — Master Handbook

> **App**: Coach — AI-enabled nutrition and training app for Mongolian users
> **Bundle ID**: com.coach.mobile
> **Monetization**: QPay (19,900 MNT/month, 149,900 MNT/year) + RevenueCat/Apple IAP
> **Last Updated**: April 2026

---

## Market Context

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Mongolian-speaking fitness enthusiasts ages 18-35
- **Secondary**: Health-conscious Mongolians starting their fitness journey
- **Psychographics**: Gym-goers, dieters, body transformation seekers, calorie counters
- **Location**: Mongolia (Ulaanbaatar primarily), Mongolian diaspora
- **Device**: iPhone (primary), Android (future)

### Market Opportunity
- Fitness culture is booming in Mongolia — new gyms opening rapidly in UB
- NO calorie tracking app recognizes Mongolian food (buuz, khuushuur, tsuivan)
- International apps (MyFitnessPal, Lose It) have zero Mongolian food database
- AI photo recognition of local food is a massive differentiator
- QPay integration removes payment friction for Mongolian users
- Telegram is hugely popular in Mongolia — bot integration is a native fit

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness (vs. Coach) |
|---|---|---|
| MyFitnessPal | Massive food database, brand recognition | No Mongolian food, no AI photo recognition, English-centric |
| Lose It! | Clean UI, good photo recognition | English only, no Mongolian food, no QPay |
| Yazio | Good meal planning | No Mongolian language, no AI coach |
| Samsung Health | Pre-installed on Samsung | No food tracking depth, no AI |
| Cal AI | AI-first food tracking | No Mongolian food knowledge, expensive |

**Coach's moat**: Only fitness app with AI recognition of Mongolian food + Mongolian voice food logging + QPay payments + AI coach with Mongolian context + Telegram bot integration.

## Phase 2: Growth (Month 1-3)

### Goals
- 2,000 downloads in first month
- 5,000 downloads by month 3
- 5% free-to-paid conversion

### Growth Channels

#### 1. TikTok / Instagram Reels (PRIMARY)

**Content Formula**:

- "What I eat in a day — tracked with Coach AI"

#### 2. Gym Partnerships

#### 3. Influencer Marketing

#### 4. Telegram Channel
