## Production Tips for Solo Founder

### Equipment Needed
- Phone with decent camera (your daily phone works)
- Natural lighting (window light is best)
- Simple tripod or phone stand (3,000-5,000 MNT from a local shop)
- Screen recording app (built into iOS/Android)

### Filming Best Practices
1. Film vertically (9:16) always
2. First 1-2 seconds must grab attention -- no slow intros
3. Show the food clearly before scanning (viewers need to recognize it)
4. The "reveal" moment is key -- pause briefly before showing calories
5. Keep text large and readable on mobile
6. Use Mongolian text overlays, not English

### Batch Recording
- Cook or buy 5 different meals on one day
- Film all 5 scans in one session
- Edit into 5 separate videos
- Schedule for the week

### Editing Tools
- CapCut (free, great for Reels/TikToks)
- Canva Video (for text overlays)
- Native TikTok/IG editors (for trending audio)
