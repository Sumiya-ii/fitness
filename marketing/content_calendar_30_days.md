## Content Production Schedule

### Weekly Batch Recording (Sunday evening or Monday morning)
- Record 3-4 food scan videos for the week
- Prepare 1-2 carousels in Canva
- Write all captions for the week
- Schedule posts using Meta Business Suite

### Daily Tasks (15-20 min)
- Post scheduled content
- Respond to all comments and DMs
- Engage with 5-10 relevant accounts
- Reshare any UGC to stories

### Batching Tips for Solo Founder
1. **Record all food scans on Sunday** -- cook/order different foods, film all at once
2. **Design all carousels on Monday** -- use templates, just swap food photos and numbers
3. **Write all captions on Monday** -- copy framework, customize per post
4. **Schedule everything by Tuesday** -- Meta Business Suite handles FB + IG
5. **TikTok posts daily** -- cannot fully schedule, but save drafts
6. **Stories are spontaneous** -- real-time app usage, polls, Q&As
